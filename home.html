About the Author

Linda L. Dunlap

Austin, TX

The entire state of Texas has been her home for all these years. Linda had several careers, always in the people business. The last one was in county law enforcement, working with jail populations. The background for her books is pulled from a rich exposure to life’s good and bad characters, some in questionable settings. Noisy, humorous, and interesting, they march through her books, each one touched with the grace of time, leaving a memory or two for readers.
