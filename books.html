The Maude Rogers Crime Novels

Maude is a near sixty year old detective who refuses to be intimidated. She has arthritic joints, a fierce nicotine habit, an obsession with her own mortality, and determined to do her job.

The Memories

Demons and witches, men and elves, and a world in jeopardy are ready to face down Breanna Ascroft. Can she find the courage within herself to save everything good in her world?

The Bridges Chronicles

Jake is a determined man who loves bridges, but he loves one thing more, his childhood love, Evie. Read The Bridges Chronicles to find out if Jake can overcome his past and find love and happiness

The People Series

A girl is found in a cabin on the Missouri prairie, nearly dead from starvation and neglect when a kind passer by took her home. This is a story of strong women who beat the odds against their survival.
